Great Divide

Great Divide is a party game where you find out how well you know your friends!

Make questions that split your friends, guess what your friends will answer, and find out how similar you are to your friends. Play with 3 to 9 of your friends for free, as much as you want! How does the game support itself, financially? It doesn't; I'm broke!
